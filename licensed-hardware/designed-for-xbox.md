# Designed for Xbox

The Designed for Xbox program exists to drive **best in class** partner products that **augment the Xbox gaming experience** across endpoints, **drive profitability**, and **preserve** the Xbox brand.

## Active Categories
- Controllers
- Audio
- Racing Wheels
- Charging
- External Storage
- Fight Sticks
- Flight Sticks
- Skins
- Mounts
- Cables
- Custom Hardware
